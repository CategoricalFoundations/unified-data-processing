=== Theoretical Justification ===

Upper bound O(n log n):
  - Batch-to-stream requires sorting events by timestamp
  - Optimal comparison sort is O(n log n)

Lower bound Ω(n log n):
  - Reduction from sorting: if transformation is o(n log n),

  - Contradicts information-theoretic lower bound

Hence: Θ(n log n) is tight.
